Modern History

Timeline - (15th Century - 20th Century)

15th - 16th Century

Renaissance and Exploration

1453: Fall of Constantinople; end of Byzantine Empire.

1492: Columbus discovers the Americas; Spanish Reconquista ends.

1498: Vasco da Gama reaches India via sea.

1517: Martin Luther's 95 Theses; start of the Protestant Reformation.

1543: Copernicus publishes De Revolutionibus; Scientific Revolution begins.

17th Century

Scientific Revolution and Political Upheaval

1607: Jamestown established in America.

1618 - 1648: Thirty Years' War in Europe.

1642 - 1651: English Civil War.

1687: Isaac Newton publishes Principia Mathematica.

1688: Glorious Revolution; constitutional monarchy in England.

18th Century

Enlightenment and Revolutions

1707: Union of England and Scotland; formation of Great Britain.

1756 - 1763: Seven Years' War; reshapes colonial empires.

1765 - 1783: American Revolution; USA gains independence.

1789 - 1799: French Revolution; end of monarchy and rise of Napoleon.

19th Century

Industrialization and Nationalism

1804: Napoleon becomes Emperor of France.

1815: Congress of Vienna; restores European balance after Napoleon.

1837 - 1901: Victorian Era; height of the Industrial Revolution.

1848: Revolutions across Europe; rise of liberal and nationalist ideas.

1857 - 1858: Indian Rebellion (Sepoy Mutiny); British Raj established.

1861 - 1865: American Civil War.

1871: Unification of Germany and Italy.

1894 - 1895: First Sino-Japanese War; rise of Japan as a global power.

20th Century

Global Conflicts and Technological Advances

1914 - 1918: World War I; Treaty of Versailles reshapes borders.

1917: Russian Revolution; rise of communism under the Bolsheviks.

1939 - 1945: World War II; Holocaust and atomic bombings.

1947: Indian independence and partition; Cold War begins.

1961: Berlin Wall constructed.

1969: Apollo 11 Moon landing.

1989: Fall of the Berlin Wall; end of Cold War.

1991: Dissolution of the Soviet Union; end of the Cold War era.
