Ancient History

Ancient history spans from the beginning of writing and recorded human history to the end of late antiquity. It explores early civilizations, the rise of empires, and cultural achievements.

Medieval History

The medieval period, or the Middle Ages, stretches from the fall of the Roman Empire to the Renaissance. It highlights feudal societies, the Crusades, and the birth of nations.

Modern History

Modern history captures the transformative events from the 16th century onwards, such as the French Revolution, industrialization, and globalization.
