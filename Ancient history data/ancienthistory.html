Ancient History Timeline (Before 500 CE)

For more information Click on the topics here.

~3100 BCE

Rise of Sumerian civilization in Mesopotamia, invention of writing (cuneiform).

~2700 BCE - 2200 BCE

Construction of the Great Pyramids of Giza in Egypt.

~2000 BCE - 1500 BCE

Rise of the Indus Valley Civilisation in South Asia.

Babylonian Empire flourishes under Hammurabi, known for his code of laws.

~1500 BCE - 1200 BCE

Vedic period in India, associated with the composition of the Rigveda.

The Mahabharata War

The Hittite Empire dominates Anatolia.

~1200 BCE - 800 BCE

Trojan War (possibly around this time, if historical).

Early Iron Age begins, leading to the spread of iron tools and weapons.

776 BCE

First Olympic Games held in ancient Greece.

~600 BCE - 500 BCE

Birth of major religions and philosophies:

  • Zoroastrianism in Persia.
  • Confucianism and Daoism in China.
  • Buddhism and Jainism in India.

509 BCE

Founding of the Roman Republic.

323 BCE

Death of Alexander the Great; start of the Hellenistic period.

221 BCE

Qin Dynasty unifies China; construction of the Great Wall begins.

27 BCE - 476 CE

Roman Empire flourishes, influencing law, culture, and architecture.
